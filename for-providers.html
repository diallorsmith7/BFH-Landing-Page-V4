FAQ

Common questions

Is it really free to list?
Yes — completely free during our beta phase in Detroit. We’ll give you plenty of notice before any pricing changes, and founding providers will always get preferential terms.
Do I need to use Calendly?
Yes. Calendly is how patients book directly with you without any back-and-forth. Calendly has a free tier that works perfectly for this.
What does “culturally competent care” mean to you?
It means you understand the lived experiences of Black families, take their concerns seriously, and create an environment where they feel seen and respected. You don’t have to be Black to qualify — but you do have to be committed.
How long does approval take?
We review applications within 48 hours on business days. You’ll hear from us either way by email.
Can I update my profile after it goes live?
Yes. Email us at hello@blkfamilyhealth.com and we’ll update your profile within 24 hours. Self-service profile editing is on our roadmap.
What specialties do you accept?
Primary care, family medicine, therapy, OB/GYN, maternal health, pediatrics, internal medicine, and chronic disease management. If you’re unsure, apply and we’ll let you know.
