Common questions

FAQs for providers

Is it really free to get listed? ▼
Yes — listing is completely free during our Detroit launch phase. We plan to introduce a premium tier later with advanced analytics and priority placement, but your basic listing will always be free.
Do I need to be a Black provider to join? ▼
No. We welcome any provider who has demonstrated commitment to culturally competent care for Black families — whether through training, lived experience, or practice philosophy. We do verify this during our review process.
How does booking work? ▼
Families click your Calendly or booking link directly from your profile. There’s no middleman — appointments go straight to your calendar. We recommend setting up a free 20-minute intro call as your first appointment type.
How long does the review process take? ▼
We typically review applications within 48 hours on business days. We verify your license, specialty, and location before publishing your profile.
Can I update my profile after it goes live? ▼
Yes. Email us at hello@blkfamilyhealth.com with any changes and we’ll update your profile within 24 hours. A self-service provider dashboard is coming soon.
What specialties do you accept? ▼
We accept all healthcare specialties — from primary care and therapy to OB/GYN, pediatrics, cardiology, dermatology, and more. If you serve Black families, we want you in our network.
